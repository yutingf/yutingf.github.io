Rachel has published in peer-reviewed journals, including the [*IMF Economic Review*](/publication/calamities-debt-and-growth/), the [*International Journal of Finance & Economics*](/publication/collateralized-borrowing-around-the-world/), the [*Review of Development Economics*](/publication/technology-adoption-and-the-middle-income-trap/), etc. She has served as an anonymous peer reviewer for journals such as *China Economic Review*, the *Journal of International Development*, the *Middle East Development Journal*, etc.
