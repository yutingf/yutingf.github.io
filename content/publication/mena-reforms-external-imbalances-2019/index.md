While some MENA economies have maintained what this Update calls \u201cunexplained\u201d current account balances for several years, fiscal policy has lost some of its historical role as a driver of the current account. In addition, the region\u2019s capacity to recirculate savings from one country to another also seems to have weakened, most notably since 2014, when the global restructuring of the oil market became abundantly apparent.
